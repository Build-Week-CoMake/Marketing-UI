What is Co-Make?

Co-Make provides an accessible and inclusive application that allows community members to escalate concerns quickly and efficiently.

How it works:

1

Create a Co-Make account, and check out the issues at hand in your community. If you don't see something you're looking for, create a new Make request!

2

Once you find the issues you're most concerned about, vote for it to be addressed by your local government.

3

Then, watch as your community becomes the voice of your town. Sit back and watch government make the decisions for the greater good, by the greater good.
